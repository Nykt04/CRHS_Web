About CRHS

A school rooted in growth, discipline, and purpose.

Castillejos Resettlement High School is a community-driven institution committed to academic excellence, values formation, and innovation for every learner.

Mission Vision Core Values School History Principal's Message

Mission

To nurture globally competitive learners who embody excellence, service, and integrity.

Vision

To become a model school that produces responsible and future-ready leaders.

Milestones

2000

Established as a community-centered secondary school in Castillejos.

2012

Expanded academic offerings and strengthened STEM and ICT initiatives.

2020

Integrated digital learning and resilient education programs.

2025

Continues to lead through innovative student support and community partnerships.
