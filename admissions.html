Admissions

Apply for a place in a school that values excellence and care.

Enrollment is open for students who are ready to grow academically, socially, and personally in a supportive community.

Enrollment Requirements

Completed application form, report card, birth certificate, and good moral character certification.

How to Apply

Visit the school office, submit your requirements, and attend the orientation and interview schedule.
